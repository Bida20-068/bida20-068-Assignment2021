WELCOME TO GABORONE BOTSWANA

Gaborone is a city situated between Kgale Hill and Oodi Hill in the south western part of botswana.Gaborone is situated 15km from the South African border.It is also home to the biggest airport in Botswana, Sir Seretse Khama International Airport.The city of Gaborone is named after Kgosi Gaborone, the leader of Batlokwa, who are settled in Tlokweng area. The indivisuals found in the city are of different culture making it not different from any other city.Western lifestlyle is taken into consideration in the city as it is soiled with pubs and luxury hotels.The outside of the city will make you think you are in a complete foreign world, silence from the city noice takes your breath away as you enter the rural Africa areas

The city of Gaborone is named after chief Gaborone of Batlokwa who led his tribe to the place in 1880.It was also a colonial fort for Cecil John Rhodes ten years after the Batlokwa moved into the place.

It then became an administrative village when Botswana moved towards aquiring independence in the early 60's.Due to its stratergic location, availability of adequate water supply from the dam and its proximity to the railway line it was chosen as the capital city of The Republic of Botswana.Initially the city was called Gaberones.

Gaborone was founded as the capital city of Botswana in 1965.It is noted that the entire city was constructed within three years.Botswana gained independence on September 30 1966 from the British Protectorate.The name was changed from Gaberones to Gaborone in 1969.

Gaborone has all the facilities found in any modern city in the world where one can enjoy their leisure time, from night clubs to the holy places of worship.There are different resturants across the city that meet the international standards, this include the likes of Ocean Basket,where one can access different sea foods.There are also fast food outlets like chicken-liken, kfc and hungry lion just to mention a few.
