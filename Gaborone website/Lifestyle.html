TOURIST ATTRACTION SITES

There are different tourists attraction sites across the city which include game reserves and parks.There is the famous Gaborone Game Reserve which shelters the native animals like wildebeest and Impala and some migratory birds.

The lion Park Resort a theme,water and animal park.It offers a water park with a wave pool,kids pool, speed and kids slides,amusement rides,voley ball court,bar and shops as well as lions.

There is also Botswana craft which is the largest handcraft center in Gaborone.We also have a courtyard resturant,conference room and host corporate events and music shows.
